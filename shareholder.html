Ownership Structure

Win-Wing Production Inc. is backed by a robust and diverse shareholder network committed to nurturing next-generation cultural exports.

Togawa Group, spearheaded by Sadaharu Togawa, holds the majority stake and provides financial stability, strategic vision, and cross-sector synergy across entertainment, tech, and communication industries.

Bushiroad, a pioneer in multimedia IP development, is our second-largest shareholder, enabling synergistic integration with major franchises and live entertainment platforms.

The remaining shares are distributed among boutique funds, private investors, and internal stakeholders—including creative directors, lead producers, and artist equity holders.
